HOW WE STARTED

Our Story

We at arojewelry. are celebrating life and love through the art & science of fine jewelry. Led by brothers Jack and Dominick Gabriel, we share a unique passion for the true value of fine jewelry and the emotions jewelry signifies. Over the past 30 years, arojewelry. has earned the reputation as being passionate, dependable, and artistic designers, creators and manufacturers. Our extensive line of timeless pieces encompasses classic and fashion-forward design, ultimately bringing together impeccable artistry with enduring technology, perfect for every unforgettable moment.
